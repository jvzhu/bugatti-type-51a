## Engine Specifications

### Power Plant
```
Configuration:    Straight-8 inline
Camshaft:         Twin overhead cam (DOHC)
Displacement:     2,262 cc (138 ci)
Bore x Stroke:    60mm × 100mm
Compression Ratio: 6.5:1
Fuel System:      Carbureted (Bugatti design)
Ignition:         Magneto-based
```

### Performance Characteristics
```
Maximum RPM:      5,500 rpm
Horsepower:       200 hp @ 5,500 rpm
Torque:           Est. 150 lb-ft @ 4,000 rpm
Power/Weight:     0.34 hp/lb (exceptional for era)
Fuel Consumption: 12-15 mpg (racing conditions)
```

## Transmission & Drivetrain

### Transmission
- **Type:** Manual
- **Gears:** 4 forward speeds
- **Gear Ratios:** 1st: 4.5, 2nd: 3.1, 3rd: 2.1, 4th: 1.4
- **Final Drive:** 3.73:1 ratio
- **Clutch:** Single-plate, mechanically operated

### Power Delivery
- Engine → Transmission → Differential → Live Axle
- No synchromesh (double-clutching required)
- Open differential (no limited-slip)

---

## Chassis & Suspension

### Frame
- **Material:** Tubular steel
- **Type:** Ladder-frame construction
- **Wheelbase:** 108 inches (2,743 mm)
- **Track (Front):** 57 inches (1,448 mm)
- **Track (Rear):** 59 inches (1,498 mm)
- **Curb Weight:** 1,540 lbs (698 kg)

### Suspension

**Front:**
- Independent double A-arm
- Transverse leaf spring
- Bugatti-designed geometry
- Ride height adjustable

**Rear:**
- Live axle (solid rear axle)
- Semi-elliptic leaf springs (longitudinal)
- Torque tube drive
- No rear anti-roll bar

### Steering
- **Type:** Worm-and-sector
- **Ratio:** Approximately 18:1
- **Turns Lock-to-Lock:** ~4.5 turns
- **Manual operation (no power steering)**

---

## Braking System

### Brake Type
- **System:** Mechanical drum brakes (all wheels)
- **Actuation:** Cable-operated
- **Brake Fluid:** None (pure mechanical)
- **Brake Drums:** Aluminum with steel linings

### Brake Specifications
- **Front Brakes:** 2-wheel independent drums
- **Rear Brakes:** Live axle drum brakes
- **Brake Fade:** Significant concern (requires management)
- **Stopping Distance:** 300 ft from 60 mph (modern test)
